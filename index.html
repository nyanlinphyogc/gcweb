The Green in Green Circle

Sustainability isn't a slogan. It's the business plan.

We are steadily converting how we power, move, and package — so growth and environmental stewardship run on the same track.

Sun to solar panels to battery storage to the production line — cleaner energy in every bottle.
01

Solar & battery energy

On-site solar generation with battery storage reduces grid dependence and cuts the carbon footprint of every bottle produced.

02

EV fleet transition

Our delivery and operations fleet is transitioning to electric vehicles, lowering emissions across the distribution chain.

03

Circular packaging

Lightweight packaging design, recycling initiatives, and responsible water sourcing — closing the loop from source back to source.

Our Journey

Three decades of building, bottle by bottle.

From a single tamarind drink to a multi-brand beverage company — every era reinvested in the next.

1996

Where it began

Our story starts with a single product: VeVe tamarind drink in a plastic can. One flavor, made for Myanmar.

Growing the family

From one brand to four

VeVe expands into juices, milk drinks, and water — joined over the years by Rocker Energy Drink, Ion Plus, and VeVe Gold.

Modern manufacturing

Built to international standards

Investment in modern filling and canning lines, strict quality systems, and a skilled local workforce raises the bar for every bottle.

Today

The green era

Solar and battery power, an EV fleet transition, circular packaging — and new launches like VeVe Premium Tamarind and Rocker Extra Strength. Thirty years in, still moving.
